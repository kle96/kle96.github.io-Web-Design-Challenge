Summary


For the Weather API Analysis, we will be exploring the relationship between the weather and location. It is well known that as you get closer to the equator, the temperature increases and gets hotter. Therefore, we want to see if we could find quantitative results that support this theory.


To find these results, we will be utilizing data on temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed over 500 cities. Our target goal is to discover if there are any relationship between these different weather features and how close the cities are to the equator. Thus, we will be utilizing the latitude of each city to determine the distance from the equator with a latitude of 0 being at the equator and +/- 90 being furthest away from the equator.
