Latitude vs. Humidity

Based on the data, it was found that the closer the cities are to the equator the hotter the city is. The chart above shows there is a parabolic relationship between the maximum temperature and the latitude. The peak temperature can be found in cities with a latitude around 20-40. Therefore, it is interesting to see that cities in the southern hemisphere tend to be hotter than those in the northern hemisphere.
