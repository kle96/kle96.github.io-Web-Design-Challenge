Latitude vs. Cloudiness

The visual shows a scatter plot with the latitude as the independent variable and cloudiness (%) as the dependent variable. A cloudiness value of 0% means that there are no clouds while a cloudiness of 100% indicates heavy clouds. As shown, there is no relationship between the latitude and the percentage of clouds as the points are randomly scattered.
